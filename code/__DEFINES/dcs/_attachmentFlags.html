ATFA_ATTACHEDThis denotes that we are attached to a supporting atom
ATFS_SUPPORTERThis denotes that we are a supporting atom to the thing attached to us
ATFS_DELETE_RECURSIVEATFS - Attachment flags for the supporting atom DEPENDING on wheter its an attached or a supporter, the bitflags change meaning If the supporter is marked for deletion/deleted , also delete the attached atoms. Default behaviour
ATFS_DROP_ATTACHEDWill detach all attached atoms and drop them on the turf
ATFS_RELAY_SIGNALSThe supporting atom shall relay signals to all its attached atoms
ATFS_PRIORITIZE_ATTACHED_FOR_HITSThis will cause all attached atoms to be checked first for hitbox collisions instead of us
ATFS_IGNORE_HITSThis will make all bullets ignore the attached atom
ATFA_HOLDINGATFA - Attachment flags for the attached atom This denotes that we are holding onto the supporting atom.
ATFA_DETACHABLEWe can be detached from the wall(either through a interaction or verb)
ATFA_EMBEDDEDThis is embedded into the wall , removeable using surgical clamps or pliers, and not by normal means.
ATFA_CLOSE_INTERACTIVEThe player must be next to the wall to interact with the attached
ATFA_EASY_INTERACTIVEPlayer has to be near the turf the attached is facing to interact with it. Default behaviour
ATFA_DIRECTIONAL_HITTABLE_STRICTThis can only be hit if the bullets come from the direction it is facing or if they are penetrating ,but otherwise not.
ATFA_DIRECTIONAL_HITTABLEThe bullet can come from any axis that shares a direction bit-flag with ourselves
ATFA_CENTER_ON_SUPPORTERWill force any hit-checking to be relatively offset by our position compared to the supporting atom.
